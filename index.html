The Problem

Limited Infrastructure

India’s EV adoption is growing, but charging stations are scarce and centralized.

Range Anxiety

EV users worry about running out of charge with limited access to chargers.

High Costs

Building large-scale public charging networks is expensive and slow.

The Solution: Chargzin

A community-powered platform where anyone can rent out their EV charger, creating a decentralized, affordable, and scalable charging network.

For EV Owners

Find nearby charging points, book instantly, and pay securely through the Chargzin app or website.

For Hosts

List your charger and earn passive income by providing charging access to EV users.
